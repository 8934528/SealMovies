Frequently Asked Questions

Is Seal Movies really free?

Yes! Seal Movies is completely free to use. We don't require any subscriptions, credit cards, or payments. This is a demonstration project for educational purposes.

How do I create an account?

Click the user icon in the top right corner and select "Register". You can use any email and password for demonstration purposes - no real registration required.

Why can't I find a specific movie?

Seal Movies is a demonstration project with a limited selection of movies. In a real implementation, the catalog would be much larger and regularly updated.
